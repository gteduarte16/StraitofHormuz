# Strait of Hormuz Oil Transit Map

Interactive visualization of global oil transit chokepoints in 2026, focused on the Strait of Hormuz, with animated trade flows, conflict timeline, and responsive map panels.

## Overview

This project maps major maritime oil chokepoints and highlights the strategic role of the Strait of Hormuz in global energy trade. It visualizes:

- Major exporters into the Strait of Hormuz
- Major importer destinations from the Strait
- Other major global chokepoints
- Animated flow lines for oil transit routes
- A timeline and status panel for the 2026 Strait of Hormuz conflict

The goal is to present a complex geopolitical and energy security issue in a clear, interactive format.

## Features

- Interactive Leaflet-based world map
- Animated inflow and outflow oil routes
- Strait of Hormuz focus layer
- Importer and exporter labels by zoom level
- Conflict timeline and status panel
- Responsive overlay panels for different screen sizes
- Comparative view of other major maritime chokepoints
- Strategic oil inventory summary

## Data Notes

This visualization presents **major exporters and major importers only**.

Some route-level information was not available in the source used. For example, destination-level flow data from the Strait of Hormuz to the Philippines was not available. Because of that, this map does not represent a complete list of all origins and destinations.
